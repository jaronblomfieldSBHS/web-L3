Welcome to Shiroquil!

The purpose of this little website is rather simple. It's to inform people about motivation or the lack thereof. It doesn't matter if you're in need of assistance or not, understanding motivation as a whole is the real goal. We aim to provide information that will help you understand how it effects you, your brain, and your livelihood. This includes little tips that will help you achieve your goal in curing or at least improving your lack of motivation! So go ahead and go through this little website at your own pace, in any order that you prefer.
